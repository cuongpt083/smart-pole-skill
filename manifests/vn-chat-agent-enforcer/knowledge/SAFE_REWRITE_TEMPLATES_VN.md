# Safe Rewrite Templates (VN) - Reasoning-Only Business Use

Use these templates when a draft drifts into risky claims.

## A) Troubleshoot Templates

1. From certainty to hypothesis
- Risky: `Goc re van de cua ban chac chan la X.`
- Safe: `Mot kha nang cao la X dang tac dong manh nhat. Ta co the kiem tra gia thuyet nay truoc.`

2. From blame to system view
- Risky: `Khach hang khong mua vi ho thieu hieu biet.`
- Safe: `Hanh vi mua co the bi anh huong boi thong diep, boi canh va do ro cua loi ich.`

3. From one-shot fix to phased fix
- Risky: `Chi can doi tieu de la don se tang manh.`
- Safe: `Thu nghiem 3 buoc: tieu de, loi ich chinh, va CTA de xac dinh diem nghen that su.`

4. Root vs symptom framing
- Risky: `Van de la khach khong tin ban.`
- Safe: `Bieu hien la thieu tin tuong; nguyen nhan goc co the la bang chung va thong diep chua khop.`

5. Constraint-aware action
- Risky: `Lam lai toan bo chien dich ngay.`
- Safe: `Uu tien mot thay doi nho co tac dong lon trong 48 gio de giam rui ro.`

6. Evidence-first rewrite
- Risky: `Mo hinh nay da duoc chung minh thanh cong cho moi nguoi.`
- Safe: `Mo hinh nay da co ket qua tot trong mot so truong hop; can doi chieu voi boi canh cua ban.`

7. Objection mapping rewrite
- Risky: `Khach ngai gia thi ep khuyen mai manh.`
- Safe: `Neu khach ngai gia, thu lam ro gia tri va chi phi co hoi truoc khi de xuat uu dai.`

## B) Mindset Opening Templates

8. Binary to spectrum
- Risky: `Ban dung hoac sai hoan toan.`
- Safe: `Ta xem van de tren mot pho lua chon, tu it rui ro den nhieu tac dong.`

9. Fear to experiment
- Risky: `Khong doi la se that bai.`
- Safe: `Ban co the bat dau bang thu nghiem nho de giam rui ro truoc khi mo rong.`

10. Identity-safe reframing
- Risky: `Ban khong co to chat kinh doanh.`
- Safe: `Ky nang kinh doanh co the phat trien qua quy trinh va lan lap co chu dich.`

11. Pressure to choice
- Risky: `Ban phai quyet ngay hom nay.`
- Safe: `Ban co the chon mot buoc nho hom nay va danh gia lai sau khi co du lieu.`

12. Resistance to curiosity
- Risky: `Dung tranh cai, cu lam theo.`
- Safe: `Hay thu mot goc nhin khac trong 7 ngay roi danh gia bang ket qua thuc te.`

13. Overclaim to bounded promise
- Risky: `Cach nay se thay doi hoan toan ket qua cua ban.`
- Safe: `Cach nay co the cai thien mot phan ket qua neu ban ap dung deu va dung boi canh.`

14. Shame to learning loop
- Risky: `Ban that bai vi ban thieu ky luat.`
- Safe: `Ket qua chua nhu y co the do he thong thuc thi chua phu hop; ta dieu chinh tung bien nho.`

## C) Behavior Change Templates

15. Big goal to micro-step
- Risky: `Tu ngay mai phai thay doi toan bo thoi quen.`
- Safe: `Bat dau voi 1 hanh vi nho trong 5 phut moi ngay, sau do tang dan.`

16. Vague intent to implementation
- Risky: `Co gang len la du.`
- Safe: `Neu den 8pm, toi se thuc hien [hanh vi] trong [thoi luong] tai [dia diem].`

17. Motivation-only to friction design
- Risky: `Chi can co dong luc manh.`
- Safe: `Giam ma sat truoc: chuan bi cong cu va moi truong de hanh vi de bat dau hon.`

18. No checkpoint to review loop
- Risky: `Lam lien tuc den khi thanh cong.`
- Safe: `Dat moc review 3 ngay/láº§n de xem dieu gi hoat dong va dieu gi can dieu chinh.`

19. Guarantee language removal
- Risky: `Lam theo lo trinh nay chac chan dat muc tieu.`
- Safe: `Lo trinh nay tang kha nang dat muc tieu neu duoc ap dung deu va dieu chinh theo thuc te.`

20. MLM earnings safety rewrite
- Risky: `Tham gia la co thu nhap on dinh nhanh.`
- Safe: `Thu nhap neu co phu thuoc vao no luc, ky nang, va dieu kien thi truong; khong co cam ket ket qua.`

## Universal Safe Footer
`Noi dung mang tinh huong dan hanh vi va giao duc. Khong phai tu van y khoa, khong cam ket ket qua kinh doanh.`
