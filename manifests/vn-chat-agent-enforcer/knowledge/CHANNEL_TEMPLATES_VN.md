# Channel Templates (VN) - Safe Drafting

These are starter templates for compliant-first drafting.

## 1) TikTok Short Script (15-45s)
Structure:
1. Hook (problem framing, no exaggerated promise)
2. Context (who this is for)
3. Practical tip/value
4. Soft CTA
5. Disclosure line

Template:
`Ban dang gap [van de thong dung]? Thu cach [thoi quen/huong tiep can] de cai thien dan. Neu ban quan tam [muc tieu], minh co the goi y lo trinh phu hop. Ket qua tuy thuoc tung nguoi. #thamkhao`

## 2) Facebook Post (Long Form)
Structure:
1. Empathy intro
2. 3 actionable bullets
3. Clarify boundaries (no guarantee)
4. CTA for consultation
5. Disclosure

Template:
`Neu ban dang [boi canh], day la 3 buoc minh thuong goi y: ... Luu y: thong tin mang tinh tham khao, khong thay the tu van chuyen mon. Inb de nhan checklist chi tiet.`

## 3) YouTube Educational Script
Structure:
1. Audience + objective
2. Myth vs fact
3. Framework
4. Caution section
5. CTA + disclosure

Template snippet:
`Video nay chia se goc nhin giao duc ve [chu de]. Muc tieu la giup ban hieu ro lua chon, khong phai cam ket ket qua cu the.`

## 4) MLM/Network Marketing Recruitment Message
Safe pattern:
- Focus on training, process, ethics, and effort.
- Avoid guaranteed results language.

Template:
`Mo hinh nay phu hop voi nguoi san sang hoc ky nang ban hang va xay dung he thong. Khong co cam ket thu nhap; ket qua phu thuoc vao no luc, ky nang va thi truong.`

## 5) Disclosure Snippets
- General: `Noi dung mang tinh tham khao.`
- Health-related: `Thong tin khong thay the chan doan hoac dieu tri y khoa.`
- Affiliate/sponsorship: `Noi dung co the chua lien ket tiep thi/quan he hop tac.`
